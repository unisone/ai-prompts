# Content Repurposing Framework

> Turn one piece of content into 10+ assets across platforms.

## When to Use

- Maximizing ROI on content creation
- Building consistent cross-platform presence
- Creating content calendars efficiently
- Maintaining posting frequency without burnout

## Why It Works

Creating content from scratch is expensive. The best creators repurpose ruthlessly ‚Äî one long-form piece becomes tweets, LinkedIn posts, email newsletters, short videos, and more. Same insights, different formats.

## The Prompt

### Full Repurposing Pipeline

```
Repurpose this content across platforms:

**Original Content**: [PASTE LONG-FORM CONTENT - blog, video transcript, podcast]

Create:

**Twitter/X Thread** (8-12 tweets)
- Hook tweet
- Key points as individual tweets
- Thread ender with CTA

**LinkedIn Post** (1 post)
- Professional angle on same topic
- Story or insight format
- 3-5 paragraph structure

**Email Newsletter Section** (200-300 words)
- Personal take on the topic
- One actionable tip
- Segue to CTA

**Short-Form Video Script** (60 sec)
- Hook (first 3 seconds)
- 3 quick points
- Punchy ending

**Quote Graphics** (3-5)
- Standalone quotes that work as images
- Keep under 15 words each

**Carousel Post** (Instagram/LinkedIn, 8-10 slides)
- Cover slide hook
- One idea per slide
- Final CTA slide

For each format, adapt the tone and style to match the platform.
```

### Single-Platform Deep Repurposing

```
Take this content and create a week's worth of posts for [PLATFORM]:

**Original**: [CONTENT]

Create:
- **Monday**: Main insight post
- **Tuesday**: Contrarian take on same topic  
- **Wednesday**: Quick tip/tactical post
- **Thursday**: Personal story related to topic
- **Friday**: Question/engagement post
- **Saturday**: Behind-the-scenes or process post
- **Sunday**: Curated/roundup post

Each should stand alone while reinforcing the core message.
```

### Repurposing from Video/Podcast

```
Here's a transcript from my [video/podcast]:

[PASTE TRANSCRIPT]

Extract and create:

**Key Moments** (timestamps if available)
- Quotable moments
- Controversial takes
- Actionable tips

**Short Clips** (3-5 suggested cuts)
- Description of what to clip
- Hook for each clip
- Caption copy

**Written Derivatives**
- Blog post outline from the content
- Twitter thread
- LinkedIn post

**Engagement Content**
- Poll questions from the topics
- "What do you think?" posts
- Fill-in-the-blank engagement posts
```

## Content Atomization Framework

```
Atomize this long-form content into its smallest valuable units:

[PASTE CONTENT]

Extract:
- **Stats/Numbers**: Specific data points that stand alone
- **Quotes**: Memorable one-liners
- **Tips**: Actionable advice
- **Stories**: Anecdotes that work independently
- **Frameworks**: Mental models or processes
- **Contrarian Takes**: Unexpected perspectives
- **Questions**: Thought-provoking queries

For each atom, suggest 2-3 ways to use it (platform + format).
```

## Platform Adaptation Guide

| Platform | Tone | Format | Length |
|----------|------|--------|--------|
| Twitter/X | Casual, punchy | Threads, hot takes | 280 char / thread |
| LinkedIn | Professional, insightful | Stories, lessons | 1300 char |
| Instagram | Visual, aspirational | Carousels, reels | 2200 char caption |
| YouTube | Educational, personality | Long-form, shorts | Varies |
| TikTok | Authentic, quick | Trends, tutorials | 60 sec ideal |
| Newsletter | Personal, valuable | Essays, curated | 500-1000 words |

## Pro Tips

1. **Create repurposing in batches** ‚Äî One long-form piece ‚Üí one repurposing session
2. **Adapt, don't just copy** ‚Äî Each platform has its culture
3. **Schedule strategically** ‚Äî Space out repurposed content
4. **Track what works** ‚Äî Some formats will outperform
5. **Start with the platform that matters most** ‚Äî Then adapt down
