# Product Mockup Generator

> Create professional product photos and mockups with AI.

## When to Use

- Product photography before physical prototype
- E-commerce listings
- Social media product shots
- Pitch decks and presentations
- Marketing materials

## Why It Works

Product photography is expensive and time-consuming. AI can generate professional-looking product shots in minutes. The key is using e-commerce and commercial photography language that AI models understand from their training data.

## The Prompt

### E-Commerce Product Shot

```
Create a professional e-commerce product photo of:

**Product**: [Detailed description of the product]
**Angle**: [Front/3/4 view/top-down/hero shot]
**Background**: [Pure white/gradient/lifestyle context]
**Lighting**: [Soft studio/dramatic/natural]
**Props**: [None/minimal styling/full scene]

Technical requirements:
- Clean, sellable appearance
- Product fills 80% of frame
- No harsh shadows on background
- Color-accurate representation
- Commercial photography quality
- Shot on Canon 5D, 100mm macro lens

Style: Amazon/Shopify product listing quality
```

### Hero Product Shot

```
Create a dramatic hero product image:

**Product**: [Product description]
**Angle**: Low angle, looking up at product (heroic perspective)
**Lighting**: Dramatic side lighting with rim light
**Background**: Dark gradient, subtle texture
**Mood**: Premium, aspirational, powerful
**Effects**: Subtle reflections, professional retouching

Style: Apple product photography
Technical: 8K, hyperrealistic, commercial quality
```

### Lifestyle Product Context

```
Create a lifestyle product photo:

**Product**: [Product description]
**Setting**: [Environment where product is used]
**User**: [Optional - person interacting with product]
**Story**: [What moment are we capturing?]
**Mood**: [Aspirational/relatable/professional/casual]

Lighting: Natural, authentic, golden hour warmth
Style: Instagram lifestyle brand photography
Feeling: "I want that life"

Technical: Shot on iPhone 15 Pro, natural colors, authentic feel
```

## Angle & Composition Guide

| Angle | Best For | Effect |
|-------|----------|--------|
| Straight-on front | Tech, packaging | Clean, informative |
| 3/4 view | Most products | Shows dimension |
| Top-down (flat lay) | Small items, collections | Overview, organization |
| Low angle (hero) | Premium products | Powerful, aspirational |
| Eye-level lifestyle | Products in use | Relatable, contextual |
| Detail/macro | Textures, quality | Craft, premium feel |

## Pro Tips

1. **Study real product photography** — Reference successful brands
2. **Match your brand aesthetic** — Consistency across all shots
3. **Show scale when relevant** — Include familiar objects
4. **Multiple angles** — E-commerce needs 4-6 views minimum
5. **Edit after generation** — AI output is starting point

## Quality Checklist

```
Before using AI product image, verify:

□ Product proportions look correct
□ No weird artifacts or distortions
□ Lighting is consistent and professional
□ Background is clean/appropriate
□ Colors appear accurate to real product
□ No text/watermarks in image
□ Resolution sufficient for intended use
□ Shadows look natural
```
