# Anti-Botspeak Guide

> Avoid phrases that instantly flag your content as AI-generated.

## When to Use

- Writing content for public consumption
- Marketing copy, blog posts, social media
- Any text that needs to sound human
- Client deliverables

## Why It Works

AI models have patterns. Readers have learned to spot them. Using these "cursed phrases" immediately signals "a bot wrote this" â€” destroying credibility and engagement. This prompt helps you get human-sounding output.

## The Prompt

### Basic Anti-Botspeak

```

AVOID these AI-sounding patterns:
- "Here's the kicker"
- "Let's dive in" / "Let's unpack this"
- "Here's the breakdown"
- "That's rare" / "And that matters"
- "It's not X, it's Y" (contrast framing)
- "You're allowed to..."
- Starting sentences with "And honestly?"
- Excessive emoji bullets (âœ… â­ ðŸŽ¯)
- Everything being "quiet" (quiet confidence, quiet truth)
- Therapist-speak ("You're not alone in feeling...")

Sound like a competent professional, not a LinkedIn influencer.
```

### Comprehensive Voice Guide

```
Write this in a genuine human voice. 

BANNED PHRASES (instant AI detection):
- "Here's the kicker" / "Here's the thing"
- "Let's dive in" / "Let's unpack this" / "Let's break this down"
- "And the best part?"
- "Here's what most people miss"
- "[X], and that matters"
- "That's rare"
- "You're not imagining it" / "You're not alone"
- "Do you want to sit with that?"
- "You're allowed to [permission-giving]"
- Forced depth ("Are you ready to go deeper?")
- Small caption formatting with labels everywhere
- Starting with "I" then immediately pivoting

INSTEAD:
- Be direct. Say what you mean.
- Use specific details over vague claims
- Vary sentence length naturally
- Have a point of view (not "on one hand... on the other")
- Sound like someone with experience, not someone performing expertise
```

## The Cursed Phrases List

### Instant Detection (Never Use)

| Phrase | Why It's Bad |
|--------|-------------|
| "Here's the kicker" | Classic GPT-ism |
| "Let's unpack this" | Overused transition |
| "That's rare" | Empty emphasis |
| "And that matters" | Appears in every AI response |
| "It's not X, it's Y" | Obvious contrast framing |
| "Here's the breakdown" | AI loves lists |
| "You're allowed to..." | Unsolicited permission |

### Structural Tells

| Pattern | Human Alternative |
|---------|------------------|
| âœ… Emoji bullets everywhere | Use sparingly or not at all |
| **Bold** every **other** word | Bold only key terms |
| Tiny labeled sections | Let ideas flow naturally |
| "First... Second... Third..." | Vary your transitions |
| Starting every paragraph same way | Mix it up |

### Therapist-Speak (Avoid)

- "You're not imagining it"
- "You're not alone in feeling..."
- "That's valid"
- "I hear you"
- "Do you want to sit with that?"

### Hype Phrases (Avoid)

- "Game-changer"
- "Revolutionary"
- "Unprecedented"
- "Here's what nobody tells you"
- "The hidden truth about..."

## Pro Tips

2. **Remove the first sentence** â€” AI often starts with throat-clearing
3. **Cut filler words** â€” "Really", "very", "actually", "just"
